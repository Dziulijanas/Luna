Introduction

Game Luna

“Luna” is a 2D platform adventure game, controlled by 2 characters and technically sharing the same screen as multiplayer, where one of the players will be involved in playing as a brother and the other player will be involved in playing as a sister. The 2 characters are young siblings that are in a search of their beloved dog called Luna through a mysterious and magical land, where it was kidnapped by the evil lord while they were sleeping. Once they are awake, they will start their journey in rescuing the dog through adventurous realm.
